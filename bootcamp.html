Bootcamp

Bootcamp is a training program that is designed to prepare you for a specific job or role. It is a short-term, intensive training program that is designed to help you learn the skills you need to succeed in a particular field.

Bootcamps are typically focused on a specific area of study, such as coding, data science, or digital marketing. They are designed to be fast-paced and immersive, with a focus on hands-on learning and real-world projects.

Bootcamps are a great way to gain new skills, advance your career, and make new connections in your field. They are also a great way to get a foot in the door at a new company or industry.
